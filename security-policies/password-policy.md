# FishForYou Global Services  
## Password Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** [01/01/2026]  
**Approved By:** Security Operations Manager  

---

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all passwords used within FishForYou Global Services provide adequate protection for company systems, data, and user accounts.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees  
- Contractors  
- Temporary staff  
- Any user accessing FishForYou systems, applications, or data  

---

## 3. Password Requirements

### **3.1 Complexity**
All passwords must:
- Be at least **12 characters** long  
- Include **uppercase**, **lowercase**, **numbers**, and **special characters**  
- Not contain personal information (names, birthdays, etc.)  
- Not reuse passwords from other accounts  

### **3.2 Password Expiration**
- Passwords must be changed every **90 days**  
- Passwords must not be reused for at least **5 cycles**  

### **3.3 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**
- MFA is required for:  
  - Email  
  - VPN  
  - Administrative accounts  
  - Remote access
  - payment methods

### **3.4 Storage and Sharing**
- Passwords must **never** be shared  
- Passwords must **never** be written down or stored in plain text  
- Use approved password managers only  

---

## 4. Account Lockout

Accounts will be locked after:
- **5 failed login attempts**  
- Lockout duration: **15 minutes**  

---

## 5. Responsibilities

Employees must:
- Protect their passwords  
- Report suspicious login activity immediately  
- Follow password creation guidelines  

IT must:
- Enforce technical controls  
- Monitor authentication logs  
- Support MFA enrollment  

---

## 6. Violations

Failure to comply may result in:
- Account suspension  
- Security investigation  
- Disciplinary action  

---

## 7. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or after major security incidents.
