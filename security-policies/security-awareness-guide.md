## 1. Introduction

This guide provides employees with essential knowledge to recognize and prevent common security threats. Every employee plays a critical role in protecting FishForYou’s systems and customer data.

---

## 2. Core Security Principles

### **2.1 Think Before You Click**
- Do not open unexpected attachments  
- Hover over links to verify the destination  
- Report suspicious emails immediately  

### **2.2 Protect Your Credentials**
- Use strong, unique passwords  
- Enable MFA on all accounts  
- Never share your login details  

### **2.3 Secure Your Workspace**
- Lock your screen when away  
- Keep sensitive documents out of sight  
- Report lost or stolen devices  

### **2.4 Safe Internet Use**
- Avoid downloading unapproved software  
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for company work  
- Only access company systems through secure channels  

---

## 3. Recognizing Common Threats

### **Phishing**
- Fake emails pretending to be trusted sources  
- Often urgent or threatening language  
- Links to fake login pages  

### **Social Engineering**
- Attackers pretending to be IT, HR, or management  
- Requests for passwords or confidential info  

### **Malware**
- Delivered through attachments, downloads, or USB devices  
- Can steal data or damage systems  

---

## 4. Reporting Procedures

Employees must report:
- Suspicious emails  
- Unexpected system behavior  
- Lost or stolen devices  
- Unauthorized access attempts  

Report via:
- **Security Operations Email:** security@fishforyou.com
